# Business Brief — FreshCo Sales Analytics

## Company Context
FreshCo Retail is a fictional multi-store grocery retail chain offering **11 product categories** — from fresh produce and dairy to confections and beverages — serving nearly **99,000 active customers**. The company generates **$4.28B in annual revenue** (after discount).

> FreshCo Retail is a fictional company created for analytical and portfolio purposes, built on the public Kaggle Grocery Sales Dataset.

## Business Problem
FreshCo's revenue is highly concentrated: the **top 5 of 11 categories contribute 53%** of total revenue, led by Confections at 13%. Leadership lacks visibility into which levers — **volume, reach, or price** — actually drive category performance. As a result:

- Promo budgets are spread evenly instead of focused on winnable categories
- Premium categories (Grain, Dairy) quietly leave mass-market demand untapped
- Exceptional customer loyalty (99.85% repeat rate in Confections) is not monetized
- High-value customers (up to $130K lifetime value) receive generic treatment

## SMART Objective
| Element | Definition |
|---------|------------|
| **S**pecific | Identify key drivers of category revenue (volume, reach, price, loyalty) and deliver data-driven growth recommendations |
| **M**easurable | Grow top-3 category revenue by 5% = **+$74.5M annually** |
| **A**chievable | Levers validated by correlation analysis on transaction-level data |
| **R**elevant | Directly addresses revenue concentration and premium accessibility gap |
| **T**ime-bound | Within **6 months**, monitored via real-time dashboard |

## Scope
**In scope:**
- Category-level revenue (after discount), units sold, unique customers, average price
- Repeat purchase behavior and top-customer lifetime value
- Variables: Quantity, Price & Discount, Unique Customers, Repeat Purchases (Loyalty)

**Out of scope:**
- Store/location-level detail
- Seasonality & time trends
- Profit margin analysis (no cost data in dataset)

## Stakeholders
| Role | Interest |
|------|----------|
| Head of Merchandising | Category investment priorities |
| Pricing Manager | Premium category price ladder |
| CRM/Loyalty Manager | Loyalty program design, VIP top-customer program |
| Finance | +$74.5M upside validation & ROI tracking |

## Success Metrics
1. Top-3 category revenue lift vs +5% target
2. Grain customer reach: 97.3K → 98.3K+
3. Loyalty program cross-category redemption rate
4. Retention of top-1% customers by lifetime value
